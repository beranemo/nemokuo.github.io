fourhandsprint studio

Our studio is located in Kaohsiung, Taiwan, and specializes in risograph printing. We have a Risograph press, and offer services for printing graphic design works, posters, magazines, art prints, CD covers and more. Currently, our studio has a total of 13 colors of ink to choose from. There will be new ink add options. In addition to risograph printing, our space also offers screen printing and binding. If you need risograph printing or screen printing services, welcome to contact us.
